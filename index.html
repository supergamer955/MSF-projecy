Medical Care
Around The World

Médecins Sans Frontières (Doctors Without Borders) is a non-profit organization that provides medical attention to anyone affected by disasters, conflict, and exclusion to healthcare. They travel all around the world to help everyone who needs medical attention, operating in more than 75 countries—mainly in conflict zones and areas affected by disease.

What they do?

People who work for Médecins Sans Frontières go all around the world giving medical attention to people in need.

LOG_02

Why do they do it?

They do what they do because they want to help people with their medical needs to make them better and not make them feel left out. Everybody should have medical attention—not only some people. There are also people without a lot of money who can't afford medical attention, and MSF is there for them.

How we can help!

We can help MSF by donating, starting fundraisers, organizing events, or doing volunteering work.

Core Principles

Medical Ethics

They prioritize patients based on need, not race or religion.

Bearing Witness

"Témoignage." They speak out about the violence they see to let the world know the truth.

Neutrality

They do not take sides in wars, which allows them to help everyone safely.

Why I Chose This Project

I chose MSF for my school presentation because their work is about basic humanity. No matter the politics, people deserve doctors. It shows that even in the middle of a war, there are people willing to help without asking questions.
