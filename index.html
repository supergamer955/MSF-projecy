medecins sans frontieres Also known as ( Doctors Without Borders ) a non for profit organization that provides medical attention To anybody affected by things such as disasters, conflict, and exclusion to healthcare. They travel all around the world to help anyone and everyone who needs medical attention. they operate in more the 75 countries. they mainly work in conflict zones( Areas affected by disaster, dissease ect).

Independence Matters

One thing I realized while researching is that MSF doesn't take government money for most of their work. This is huge because it means they don't have to take sides—they just help whoever is hurting.

Rapid Response

When an earthquake hits or a war starts, they’re usually on the ground within hours. Their logistics system is actually insane—they have massive warehouses ready to ship hospitals in boxes.

The Challenges

It's not all smooth. Between climate change making diseases spread faster and hospitals being targeted in wars, the team faces some pretty heavy risks every day.
