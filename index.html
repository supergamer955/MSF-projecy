Medical Care
Around the world

Médecins Sans Frontières (Doctors Without Borders) is a non-profit organization that provides medical attention to anyone affected by disasters, conflict, and exclusion to healthcare. They travel all around the world to help everyone who needs medical attention, operating in more than 75 countries.

Explore Report

What they do?

People who work for Médecins Sans Frontières go all around the world giving medical attention to people in need.

Why do they do it?

They want to help people with their medical needs. Everyone should have medical attention—not only some people. Many cannot afford care, and MSF fills that gap.

How we can help!

We can help MSF by donating, starting fundraisers, organizing events, or doing volunteering work.
